## Source materials from the base study

The 20 modelling exercises, the original LLM-generated UML diagrams, and the expert assessments used in this replication are taken from
the publicly available replication package of the base study:

- https://doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.25434550
- https://doi.org/10.6084/m9.figshare.25316440

To respect attribution and ensure reviewers can verify that our inputs are byte-identical to the originals, we do not redistribute these 
materials in this repository.

## Reproducing the study

- Our PlantUML transcriptions of the human-created diagrams are included in this repository under `data/diagrams_human_plantuml/`
